Made to Order

every piece is an original. In collaboration with some of the most talented craftspeople from across the British Isles, all orders are made to your exact specifications and style. From Quick Ship table lamps and custom-sized furniture, all the way to entirely bespoke creations, every product is made to order.

Since we first started, the painting studio has been central to our designs. It's there that we've spent three decades building on the craft of hand painted finishes, developing our own unique library. Each patination takes weeks to develop, sampling layers of colour up and down from light to dark and back again, in search of the perfect and unique Porta Romana finish.

This is Living

The living room is often the hub of the home, where you gather with family and friends to relax and entertain. Beautiful living room furniture can make it the showcase of your home. Ethan Allen has living room furniture sets that work in every domain. From traditional to contemporary to coastal-inspired designs, define your look with customizable sofas, sectionals, and recliners in hundreds of fabrics. Pull the space together with our selection of coffee tables, side tables, and living room chairs. All the finishing touches are here. Start creating your favorite family hangout today.

Create a Cozy Corner

Beds can make a bedroom layout feel very linear and square. You can prevent this by placing a comfortable upholstered chair in one of the corners. This softens the corner of the room and encourages circulation. If there's room, add a small side table, some artwork and an ottoman to make it a cozy and inviting place to relax.
